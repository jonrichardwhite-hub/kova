Usage rules
CLEAR SPACE
Maintain padding equal to the height of the vertical bar of the mark on all sides. Do not crowd with other elements.
MINIMUM SIZE
Mark: 24px tall minimum. Wordmark: 14px cap height minimum. Lockup: 32px tall minimum.
DO NOT
Recolor the mark beyond approved variants. Stretch, skew, rotate, add shadows, or place on visually busy backgrounds without contrast protection.

16 — Marketing site

Feature pair

Used on the Product page to introduce the platform and the kiosk side by side. Tag pill, title, paragraph, then a checked feature list.

PRESCRIPTION PROCESSING

Reads the full script, not the pair.

Looks across the patient's complete medication list, not just the new pair, before flagging anything as safe or risky.

  • Interaction checks across the full medication list, not pairwise
  • Dose verification with renal and hepatic adjustment
  • Look-alike and sound-alike (LASA) detection
  • Therapeutic duplication and compounding calculations
CLINICAL INTELLIGENCE

Judgment work, documented.

The harder, ambiguous work that pharmacists actually train for. Every recommendation comes with the reasoning trail behind it.

  • Prescriber intervention letters
  • Drug information on demand
  • Aged care medication review
  • Pharmacogenomics interpretation
